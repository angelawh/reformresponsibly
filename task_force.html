What?

A group of students and faculty who will work together to Honor Committee investigation processes and policies, Honor Code Constitution language and academic integrity standards, Honor Committee penalties, and Honor Committee membership and the Committee’s relationship to the Undergraduate Student Government.

When?

The group will meet throughout next semester, Spring 2018.

Why Will We Be Effective?

This task force does not attempt to box anyone out of the conversation. Through this referendum process, Patrick and Soraya have proven they are committed to collecting student opinion and translating it in Constitutional changes. They can bring what they have learned through the USG Academics Subcommittee on the Honor System’s fall inquiry to the spring task force, while pulling in other stakeholders to offer expertise and ensure the Code at which we arrive at the end of the task force process reflects the values of the student body and faculty.

About us

We are the opposition party against the Honor Committee reform. The party is headed by Carolyn Liziewski, Chair of the Honor Committe, and Liz Haile, the Clerk of the Honor Committee.
